[ Whale | ... | perpendicular Beam ] -> [ perpendicular Whale | ... | perpendicular Beam ]

(Remove beams)
[ Beam ] -> [ ]

Horizontal [ > Crate | ... | Crate ] -> [ > Crate | ... | > Crate ]
