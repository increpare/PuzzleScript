late [ RightB Dude ] [ LeftB Rabbit ] [ LeftB Fox ] -> [ RightB Dude ] [ LeftB ] [ LeftB Fox ] sfx0 message Fox eats rabbit! Chomp!

[ Water ] -> [ random Water ]
