This complete PuzzleScript game involves collecting cubes and placing them on targets. The player, represented by "P", can move cubes "C" onto the target "X". The game comprises walls "#" and backgrounds "." to define the play area. The player wins when all cubes are on targets, with levels designed to require strategic movement and cube placement.
