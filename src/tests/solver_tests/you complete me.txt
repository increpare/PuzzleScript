======
RULES     
======     

[> player | wall] -> cancel
late up [SUP] [|player] -> [SUP] [ice|player]
late down [SDOWN] [|player] -> [SDOWN] [ice|player]
late left [SLEFT] [|player] -> [SLEFT] [ice|player]
late right [SRIGHT] [|player] -> [SRIGHT] [ice|player]

up [SUP]  [Player ice | no wall ] -> [SUP] [ > Player ice |  ] again
down [SDOWN]  [Player ice | no wall ] -> [SDOWN] [ > Player ice |  ] again
left [SLEFT]  [Player ice | no wall ] -> [SLEFT] [ > Player ice | ] again
right [SRIGHT]  [Player ice | no wall ] -> [SRIGHT] [ > Player ice |  ] again


[marker]->[]


[ > player ] -> [ > player ] again
up [ > player ] -> [ > player sup ]
down [ > player ] -> [ > player sdown ]
left [ > player ] -> [ > player sleft ]
right [ > player ] -> [ > player sright ]

[ stationary player ] -> cancel
