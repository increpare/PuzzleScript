======
RULES
======


up [ stationary moveup car | Wall ] -> [ car | wall] 
down [ stationary movedown car | Wall ] -> [ car | wall] 
left [ stationary moveleft car | Wall ] -> [ car | wall] 
right [ stationary moveright car | Wall ] -> [ car | wall] 


up [ stationary moveup car | car no moveup ] -> [ car | car] 
down [ stationary movedown car | car no movedown ] -> [ car | car] 
left [ stationary moveleft car | car no moveleft ] -> [ car | car] 
right [ stationary moveright car | car no moveright ] -> [ car | car] 


up [ stationary moveup car | Pedestrian ] -> [ skid_n | crash_s ]  sfx0
down [ stationary movedown car | Pedestrian ] -> [ skid_s | crash_n ] sfx0
left [ stationary moveleft car | Pedestrian ] -> [ skid_w | crash_e ]  sfx0
right [ stationary moveright car | Pedestrian ] -> [ skid_e | crash_w ] sfx0


up [ stationary moveup car | no obstacle ] -> [ | action moveup car ] again sfx1
down [ stationary movedown car | no obstacle ] -> [ | action movedown car ] again sfx1
left [ stationary moveleft car | no obstacle ] -> [ | action moveleft car ] again sfx1
right [ stationary moveright car | no obstacle ] -> [ | action moveright car ] again sfx1


up [ > player | car ] -> [ > player | movedown car_v ] again
down [ > player | car ] -> [ > player | moveup car_v ] again
left [ > player | car ] -> [ > player | moveright car_h ] again
right [ > player | car ] -> [ > player | moveleft car_h ] again

[ > player | wall ] -> cancel

late [ car move no player ] -> [ car ]
